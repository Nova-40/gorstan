# Gorstan Chronicles — Introduction

Gorstan is a meta-fictional multiverse governed by a mysterious lattice. Key figures: Ayla (the fabric of reality), Morthos (demonic engineer), Al (multiversal guardian), Dominic (sentient fish), Mr. Wendell (shape-shifting antagonist). The Lattice holds memories; books in the world reveal deeper lore. All content modifications must not contradict canonical character roles and relationships.
